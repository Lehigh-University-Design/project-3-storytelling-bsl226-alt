FIFTH & FINAL RING

Kobe led the Lakers to a back-to-back championship, securing the title in a Game 7 victory over the rival Boston Celtics. He earned another Finals MVP by averaging 35.4 points, 7.2 rebounds, and 5.3 assists.

GAME 7 HEROICS

In the deciding Game 7 of the 2010 Finals, despite a poor shooting night, he grabbed 15 rebounds and created opportunities from the free-throw line to help secure the win.

METTA WORLD PEACE'S CLUTCH SHOT

In the same Game 7, a crucial three-pointer by Metta World Peace helped the Lakers secure the championship.
