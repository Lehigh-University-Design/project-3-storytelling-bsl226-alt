61-PT GAME

On February 2, 2009, Bryant scored 61 points against the New York Knicks, setting an arena record at Madison Square Garden.

"JOB NOT FINISHED"

After going up 2-0 in the 2009 Finals, Bryant famously told the media, "Job not finished," setting the tone for the rest of the series and his continued pursuit of more championships.

REVENGE ON THE MAGIC

Bryant led the Lakers to avenge their 2008 Finals loss, beating the Orlando Magic in five games to win his fourth NBA title. He also earned a Finals MVP Award after averaging 32.4 points, 5.6 rebounds, and 7.4 assists in the Finals
