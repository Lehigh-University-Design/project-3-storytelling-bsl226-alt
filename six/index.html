60-PT FINAL GAME

Kobe Bryant's final NBA game, a fitting farewell, was on April 13, 2016, where he scored 60 points in a come from behind Lakers win against the Utah Jazz, capping off his legendary career.

"MAMBA OUT"

He ended the game and the post-game speech by famously saying, "Mamba out," and blowing a kiss to the crowd. A poetic way for Kobe to say goodbye to the game he loved.
